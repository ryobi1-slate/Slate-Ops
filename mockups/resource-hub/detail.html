◆ Slate-authored notes

Read this before you torque step 6

The vendor's bracket spec calls for 22 ft-lb on the rear feet. On 144 HR we've cracked two paint coats at that value — drop to 18 ft-lb and re-check at delivery. Confirmed with Flatline on Apr 28.

For the front feet, the supplied M8 hardware is short by ~3mm once the headliner clip is in place. Use the longer M8x35 from the Slate consumables drawer (bin C-04).
